Layer D — Definitions (Arendt's foundations)
D1 Arbeit (Labor) Cyclical, consumptive activity tied to biological necessity. Produces nothing durable. Corresponds to animal laborans. VA-D1-Arbeit.md draft
D2 Herstellen (Work) Fabrication — produces durable objects that outlast the act of making. Builds the human Welt. Corresponds to homo faber. VA-D2-Herstellen.md draft
D3 Handeln (Action) The distinctly human activity of beginning something new in the presence of others. Requires plurality. Lives only in the public realm. VA-D3-Handeln.md draft
D4 Die Welt (The World) The durable artificial world built by Work. The precondition for a public realm and for Action. Distinguished from Earth and Universe. VA-D4-Welt.md draft

Layer A — Attacks (where Marx goes wrong)
A1 The Conflation Marx does not distinguish Arbeit (D1) from Herstellen (D2). His "labor" collapses both. The system is built on an ambiguous foundation. VA-A1-Conflation.md draft
A2 Glorification of the Lowest Marx elevates Labor — the activity humans share with animals — to the defining human characteristic. Animal laborans becomes the ideal. VA-A2-Glorification.md draft
A3 Labor Theory of Value By grounding value in Labor, Marx ties all value to a cyclical, consumptive process. He cannot account for durability or world-building. VA-A3-LaborValue.md draft
A4 Freedom Mislocated For Marx, freedom lies beyond necessity. For Arendt, freedom is Action (D3) — not the absence of necessity. Freedom becomes permanently deferred. VA-A4-Freedom.md draft
↓ ↓ ↓
Layer L — Consequences (lemmas)
L1 Worldlessness If all human activity is Labor, nothing durable is produced. The World (D4) disappears. Society becomes a cycle of production and consumption — Weltlosigkeit. VA-L1-Weltlosigkeit.md draft
L2 Public Realm Dissolves The public realm requires a durable world as its stage. Without the World (L1), politics becomes mere administration of economic processes. VA-L2-PublicRealm.md draft
L3 Action Becomes Impossible Action (D3) requires the public realm. Without it, humans can only labor and consume — they cannot initiate, appear, or act politically. VA-L3-ActionImpossible.md draft
L4 Communist Ideal Inverted Marx's classless society is not liberation but the perfection of animal laborans. Abundance without world. Plenty without durability. VA-L4-CommunistIdeal.md draft
↓ ↓ ↓
Layer T — Theorem
T Marxism destroys the conditions for political freedom By centering all activity on Labor and erasing D1/D2/D3, Marxism cannot theorize the World, the public realm, or Action. The result is the triumph of animal laborans: rich in consumption, poor in world, incapable of politics. VA-T-MainCritique.md draft
